“Sea Wall” Shifting Library Project: Anchoring our legal resources!

We're going to create a dedicated "Seawall" section to prominently feature our admiralty and maritime law collection, enhancing visibility and creating a focused space for our new LL.M. program about the law of the seas. This initiative aims to enhance discoverability and create a specialized hub for researchers and practitioners in this field.
